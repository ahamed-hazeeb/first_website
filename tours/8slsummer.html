Sri Lanka Summer Holiday Package

​Enjoy a rejuvenating vacation during the bright summer with our exclusive summer holiday package in Sri Lanka. Awaken your spirits amidst the luscious greenery in the central hills while relaxing in the soothing atmosphere. Glorious water falls, purest air to breath and the breathtaking views would just capture your heart. Then move to an enchanting beach just to relax in the golden shore either enjoy some adventures water sports as per your choice. Ideal holiday tour package to relax, heal and grab your spirits back after all those stressful times in life.

Day 1: Colombo Airport - Pinnawala - Kandy

Meet your English speaking Chauffeur Guide on arrival. Transfer to a Kandy, On the route visit the elephant orphanage in Pinnawala. About 60 pachyderms live in the orphanage. Many elephants living here are babies, found, abandoned or orphaned in the wild. They are cared, fed and trained by the wild life authorities. You are suggested to visit during the feeding times, when one will have the opportunity of seeing the baby elephants being bottle-fed. You can also accompany the elephants to a river close-by and watch the elephants having their daily bath. Tour the temple of the tooth relic in evening and thereafter witness a cultural show featuring conventional Sri Lankan dancing. Overnight stay at in Kandy

Day 2: Kandy City Tour

Venture on a city tour after breakfast. Kandy, the last capital of the Sri Lankan kings, has been declared a world heritage site. The name Kandy conjures visions of splendour and glory. Many of the legends, traditions and folklore are yet lively and vibrant here. Drive around the Kandy lake constructed by the last sinhala king Sri Wickrama Rajasinghe in 1806. Tour the arts & crafts centre and a gem museum and a lapidary. In the evening go to the peradeniya botanical gardens. This botanical garden was first made as a pleasure garden by a sinhala king and was expanded by the British. It stretches in 147 acres and provides an amazing variety of trees, plants and flowers. Overnight stay at in Kandy

Day 3: Kandy - Nuwara Eliya

After taking breakfast move on to Nuwara Eliya. En route tour a tea plantation and a tea factory, where the best tea in the world is produced. You will be apprised of all the process of manufacturing tea. You can also watch how tea is graded. Taste pure Ceylon tea in the factory.
Nuwara Eliya, thanks to its invigorating mountain climate and scenery, is sri Lanka's most popular hill resort. Nuwara Eliya is also the heart of sri Lanka's tea country producing spectacular share of the world's best tea. Pidurutalagala (8282 feet), Sri Lanka's highest mountain, is located here. Nuwara Eliya also holds one of the finest 18 hole golf courses in south Asia. Overnight stay at in Nuwara Eliya.

Day 4: Nuwara Eliya - Horton Plains

Leave the hotel early morning to visit Horton Plains in the hills. Trek to the beautiful Bakers’ Fall and the proverbial ‘World’s End’ offering the best panoramic views on the island.
Overnight stay at in Nuwara Eliya.

Day 5: Nuwara Eliya - Bentota

​After Breakfast visit Nuwara Eliya city tour including Sita Eliya temple, therefore proceed to Bentota via Kithulgala, En route you will be able to view Devon’s water fall & sait clairs’s water falls which are breathtaking & rich in beauty You can also do some white water rafting activities at Kithulgala. Overnight stay at in Bentota

Day 6: Bentota

After spend the day leisure on the Beach OR Experience some water sports activities Over night stay in Bentota

Day 7: Bentota - Colombo

After Breakfast transfer to Colombo, Colombo sightseeing and shopping.Over night stay in Colombo

Day 8: Colombo - Airport

After Breakfast you will be driven back to Colombo airport for your onward flight with happy Sri Lankan memories. End of Tour
