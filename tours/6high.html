Sri Lanka Highlights Tour

Discover the wonderful holiday destination Sri Lanka with our exclusive tour package of 06 days. The tour would cover up the most popular tourist attractions in Sri Lanka inclusive of the important historical, cultural and religious sites. In addition, explore the nature and unique wild life in Sri Lanka by visiting the most beautiful cities in the island, and the most famous wild life sanctuary as well. As a whole, this special tour package can be called as Sri Lanka at a glance.

Day 1: Arrival - Colombo

You will be welcomed by Sanki Leisure representative at the airport before you are transferred to Colombo. After checking in at the hotel, you will go on an afternoon city tour of Colombo. You will return to the hotel for dinner and overnight at hotel

Day 2: Colombo - Polonnaruwa

After breakfast proceed to Polonaruwa the second capital of Sri Lanka from the 11th to the 13th century AD. This ancient city is today one of the most beautiful centers of this island's cultural heritage. We will also visit the remains of the Royal Palace and Parakrama Samudra, a huge man made water reservoir. we will check in at the hotel for dinner and overnight stay at Polonnaruwa

Day 3: Sigiriya - Dambulla - Kandy

Breakfast at the hotel will be followed by a visit to Sigiriya Rock Fortress. Having climbed the rock, you will encounter the remains of the Palace and its surroundings. En route to Kandy, we will visit the Dambulla Rock Cave Temple and a Spice Garden Matale. ​
We will check in at the hotel before an afternoon visit to the Temple of the Tooth and a Kandyan cultural dance performance. At the end of the day, we will return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay at Kandy.

Day 4: Kandy - Nuwara Eliya

After breakfast at the hotel. On the way to Nuwara Eliya, we will take the Upper Lake Drive and visit the Royal Botanical Gardens at Peradeniya, the Elephant Orphanage at Pinnawela and a Tea Plantation, to see women plucking the‘Green Gold’ tea leaves. Upon arrival at Nuwara Eliya, we will take a late afternoon tour of Little England before dinner and check in at the hotel. Overnight stay at Nuwara Eliya

Day 5: Nuwara Eliya - Yala

We depart for Yala after breakfast. We will go on an afternoon visit to Yala National Park or Bundala Wildlife Park. You will see a vast array of animals including deer, peacock, elephant, wild buffalo and the lucky visitors may get to see a leopard or bear. At the end of the tour, you will check in at the hotel for dinner and overnight stay at Tissamaharama

Day 6: Yala - Galle - Colombo

After breakfast, you will visit the Galle Dutch Fort before your return to Colombo, transfer to airport or drop off at a beach extension hotel of your choice
